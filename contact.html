FAQs

Common Questions

Do you deliver across all of Ranchi? +

Yes! We deliver across all major areas of Ranchi within a 10 km radius, including Lalpur, Kanke Road, Doranda, Ashok Nagar, Harmu, Booty More and more.

How does live tracking work? +

After placing your order, you'll receive a unique Order ID. Visit our Tracking page and enter your ID to see your delivery agent's real-time location on a live map, with status updates.

Can I book a table in advance? +

Absolutely! Use the contact form above or call +91 98765 43210. We recommend booking 24 hours in advance for weekends and 2 hours for weekdays.

Do you offer catering services? +

Yes, we cater for corporate events, weddings, birthdays, and any celebration. Email us at info@thesampleone.com with your requirements and we'll create a custom menu.

The Story of The Sample One

Founded in 2018 by Chef Arjun Mehta, THE SAMPLE ONE began as a dream to bring world-class dining to the people of Ranchi, Jharkhand. What started as a 20-seat restaurant on Main Road has grown into the city's most beloved culinary institution.

Our philosophy is simple: great food starts with great ingredients. We source all our produce from local Jharkhand farmers, supporting the community while ensuring the freshest flavours on your plate.

Every dish on our menu is a story — of tradition, of innovation, and of an unwavering commitment to excellence. From our legendary Butter Chicken to our fusion Gulab Jamun Cheesecake, each creation is a masterpiece.
